Hello,

My name is Vitaliy Porsnev, I was born in a small European country called Lithuania, in the capital city - Vilnius. Both of my parents are coming from Russia, so I have been living in bilingual setting since the early childhood. Additionally, since pre-school I started learning English language, and thanks to my huge interest in travelling, cars, games, technology and hundreds of hours spent on the internet reading and watching everything concerning those topics, I was able to easily communicate in English by the time I started my high-school.

My experience in warehousing and gastronomy in Lithuania matured me in business atmosphere and handling stressful situations. It gave me the self-confidence I needed to travel abroad in order to broaden my knowledge and skills and get the international experience.

Recently, with the help of online studying plaforms like Udemy and Codeacademy I decided to learn additional, such useful in modern age subjects as software and web development. This very web site you are browsing right now is the first fruit of my studies.

In my high-school years I was very energetic and creative and have been a presentor for every school event and a member of Vilnius actors agency. And therefore, my first ever job was acting. I had a few roles in multiple advertisements and in full-lenght film by Oscar-winning italian director Gabriele Salvatores called "Siberian Education".
